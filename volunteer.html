If you would like to become involved with the activities of CIAS there are many opportunities for volunteer work in areas such as market research, fundraising and public relations. There are also possibilities to expand your network, learn new skills and make new friends!

Please send us an email cias@utoronto.ca describing how you could help.

The future of the CIAS depends on people like you who care about the region. We would like to establish a fund which will enable us to assist deserving scholars who would not otherwise be able to travel to Toronto. Your tax deductible contribution to Central and Inner Asia Studies will be warmly appreciated. Please send your cheque or money order payable to "University of Toronto: CIAS" to:
